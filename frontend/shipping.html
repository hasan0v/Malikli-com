Shipping FAQ

What shipping services do you offer?

We offer multiple shipping options including International EMS (Express Mail Service), Priority and Non-Priority International Parcels. Domestic delivery within Belarus takes 1-3 business days, while international shipping ranges from 7-30 days depending on destination and service type.

Do you provide tracking information?

Yes, all shipments include full tracking from dispatch to delivery. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped, allowing you to monitor your package's progress throughout its journey.

What are the delivery timeframes?

Delivery times vary by destination and service: EMS to Europe (7-30 days), North America (7-30 days), Asia (7-30 days), Australia (7-30 days), and Russia (7-30 days). Standard parcels take longer, ranging from 7-30 days depending on priority level and destination.

Are there any customs duties or taxes?

Customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations and the value of your order. These fees are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping costs. Processing through customs may also affect delivery times.

What factors can affect delivery times?

Several factors can impact delivery times including customs clearance, remote destinations, national holidays, weather conditions, and peak shipping seasons. We recommend allowing extra time during holiday periods and for deliveries to remote areas.

Which countries do you ship to?

We ship to over 140 countries worldwide. Neighboring countries like Russia typically receive standard delivery (7-30 days), while more distant locations may take 7-30 days for remote areas. Contact us if you're unsure about delivery to your specific location.
